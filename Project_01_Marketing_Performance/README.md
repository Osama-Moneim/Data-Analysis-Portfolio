# Marketing Performance Analysis (2022–2024)

## Business Objective
Evaluate multi-channel marketing performance to optimize budget allocation and
improve acquisition efficiency.

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue: $1.07B
- Marketing Spend: $86.3M
- ROAS: 12.46
- CPA: $7.83
- Conversion Rate: 10%

## Key Insights
- Email is the highest-efficiency channel by ROAS
- TikTok, Instagram, and Google Ads generate the highest revenue
- Desktop users convert more efficiently than mobile
- VIP and Loyal segments drive the largest revenue share

## Recommendations
- Reallocate 10–15% of budget to high-ROAS channels
- Optimize underperforming campaigns
- Improve mobile conversion experience
